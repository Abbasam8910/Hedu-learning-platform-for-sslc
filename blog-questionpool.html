SSLC Question Pool (English medium & Malayalam Medium)

  • Abbas am
  • Feb 1, 2021
  • 0 Comments

The State Council of Educational Research & Training (SCERT) Kerala has released the Kerala SSLC Question Pool for those students who are studying in Class 10. With the help of Kerala SSLC Question Pool, candidates can get an idea about the answers of the questions that have been studying in Class 10th. The main aim of the Kerala SSLC Question Pool is to bring clarity about the Answers. Kerala SSLC Question Pool is issued by the SCERT Kerala every year. It is advised to the candidates to download Kerala SSLC Question Pool as given below in the links for their preparation purposes
