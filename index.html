Simple Education Tips

Organize your study space

An organized and clean study space is one of the essentials in order to study with full concentration and without any interruption. There is a popular saying, 'a cluttered desk is a sign of a cluttered mind'.

Use flow charts and diagrams

Flowcharts can be an effective and efficient. When designing and creating a flowchart, bear in mind that the diagram needs to be easy to understand.

Practice on old exams Questions

Use past papers to make sure that you are managing your time well. Set yourself a time frame to complete each question
